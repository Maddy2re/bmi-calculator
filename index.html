A (Body Mass Index) calculator is a tool used to assess whether an individual’s body weight falls within a healthy range based on their height and weight.

It is widely used by healthcare professionals and individuals to determine weight categories that may indicate health risks.

Categories:

Underweight: BMI less than 18.5. This can signal malnutrition, an eating disorder, or other underlying health issues.

Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9. This indicates a healthy balance of weight and height.

Overweight BMI 25–29.9. This can signal overeating, a hormonal imbalance, a metabolic disorder, or other underlying health issues. Excess weight like this may lead to future health problems.

Obesity BMI 30 or above. This signals severe overeating. This is linked to a higher risk of conditions like heart disease, diabetes, and stroke.
